Who we are?

We started this organisation Make A Difference in 2015 with the aim to help village students. Having seen many village students dropping their studies after high school and having dilemma of what to study after school and ending up in wrong platform, we created this portal to connnect them with various organisation and institution in their district and giving them a guidance and skills at low or free of cost.

Education is the movement from dark to light

We offer them a good learning material which helps them get through their Entrance examination and also to prepare for their board. And we recently launched online classes because a virus should not change a child's future.

Sports

Not only Education, we also help to create a good sportsman by helping to connect with sports organisation and updating with the district, state,National level sports event.
